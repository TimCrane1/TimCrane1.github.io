Medical Towers at Riverplace

Medical Towers at River Place is located at the corner of River Place Blvd. and FM 2222 at the entrance to the prestigious River Place neighborhood. Through a collaborative process with The City of Austin, The Tower was developed in a park- like, natural environment setting with numerous heritage live oak trees with West Austin hill country views along a designated Texas Hill Country view Corridor. Medical Towers at River Place is home to nine highly renowned specialized medical practices serving West Austin.

  • 6611 River Place Blvd., Austin, Texas
  • Completed: Spring 2014
  • 36,000 SF Specailized Medical: ER, advanced imaging, rehabilitation, biomechanics, podiatry, immunology, OB/GYN, mental health, compounding pharmacy, pain management, physical therapy
  • Team: DPR Construction, BSA Life Structures Design, Stantec Civil Engineering
