About

Johnson Trube & Associates (JTA) is a full service real estate consulting, brokerage, and development firm in Austin. The founding partners, Edward Johnson and John Trube, have a combined 45 years of experience in public service real estate and private sector real estate development. Their backgrounds cover everything from the ground up development of government-owned and private sector real estate projects to deferred maintenance and energy efficiency upgrades to existing government-owned facilities.
