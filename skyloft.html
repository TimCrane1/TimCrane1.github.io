Skyloft

Skyloft is a high-rise luxury student housing two blocks from The University of Texas at Austin. The 18 story building has first class amenities that include a rooftop pool complete with a Jumbotron, multiple study rooms, rooftop fitness center overlooking downtown, and an outdoor study lounge on the first floor.

  • 218,992 SF, 212 units, 674 beds
  • Delivering: Summer 2018
  • Team: JE Dunn Construction, STG Design, Mark Hart Architecture, and Wuest Group
